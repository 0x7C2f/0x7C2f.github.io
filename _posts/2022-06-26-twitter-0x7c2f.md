All of this said:  
- Don't put in a controlled app, what you can put in a locally stored FOSS app.  
- Don't put in a FOSS app what you can write on paper.  
- Don't write when you can say.  
- Don't say when you can wink.  
- Don't wink when you can stay quiet.  
  
Be safe.  
Destroy records.
